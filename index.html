About

Hello, my name is Paul, and I'm a passionate data analyst with a degree in economics and statistics. I use software such as SQL, Python, Excel, and Tableau to effectively analyze data and visualize the results in a clear and understandable way in order to deliver high quality analysis to clients and to exceed expectations.

Throughout my academic journey, I have gained a solid understanding of the fundamental theories and principles of statistics, mathematics, and computer science. Additionally, I have honed my technical skills through my IBM Data Analyst Specialization and the various projects that I have done on my own.

I am a problem solver with a strong attention to detail, and I have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, which have enabled me to work effectively in teams and successfully collaborate with team members.

Overall, I believe that my professional background and personal qualities make me a strong candidate for any position that requires data management and analytical skills.
